## Key to Personalization at Scale

**The trick:** Personalize the ANGLE, not every word

```
Template stays same (saves time)
Angle matches their amenities (increases relevance)
Numbers change by location (increases ROI proof)

Result: Looks personalized, takes 18 seconds per email
```

**Example:**

Generic (Low conversion):
```
Subject: Increase your Airbnb bookings
Body: We help hosts get more bookings with better photos
```

Personalized (High conversion):
```
Subject: John: Your hot tub photos are costing you $8,200/year
Body: I looked at your NYC property. You have a hot tub but no clear photos. Properties WITH hot tub photos get 68% conversion. That's $8,200/year difference.
```
