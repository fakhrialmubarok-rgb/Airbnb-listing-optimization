## Step-by-Step: Connect Wise to Stripe

### Step 1: Go to Stripe Settings
1. Open: https://dashboard.stripe.com
2. Click: **Settings** (⚙️ icon)
3. Click: **Connected accounts** OR **Bank accounts and transfers**

### Step 2: Look for Wise Option
In the bank accounts section, look for:
- "Add connected account" or
- "Connect external account" or
- "Wise" / "TransferWise" option

### Step 3: Click Wise/TransferWise
- Find and click the Wise option
- Stripe will show: "Connect to Wise"

### Step 4: Authorize
1. Click: "Connect to Wise"
2. You'll be redirected to Wise login
3. Log in with your Wise credentials
4. Wise will ask: "Allow Stripe to connect?"
5. Click: "Authorize" or "Allow"

### Step 5: Confirm in Stripe
- Back in Stripe dashboard
- You should see: "Wise account connected" ✅
- Or: Status shows "Active"

### Step 6: Set as Payout Method
- In Stripe settings
- Make sure Wise account is set as: **Default payout method**
- Save ✅

---

## What to Do If You Can't Find Wise Option

If Stripe dashboard doesn't show Wise:

### Method A: Add via Bank Account Section
1. Go: Settings → Bank accounts and transfers
2. Click: "Add bank account"
3. Look for: "Connect Wise" or "TransferWise" option
4. Click it
5. Authorize

### Method B: Manual - Add Your Wise Details
1. In Wise, get your bank details:
   - Go to Wise app
   - Go to: "Receive money"
   - Select: Indonesia
   - Copy your Wise account details
   
2. In Stripe:
   - Add bank account manually
   - Use those Wise details
   - Save

### Method C: Contact Support
- If you can't find option: https://dashboard.stripe.com/contact
- Tell them: "I have Wise account, need to connect for payouts"
- They'll help
