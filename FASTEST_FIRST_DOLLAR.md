**Don't panic. This is normal.**

**Why no replies?**
- They didn't see the email (need to retry)
- Wrong subject line (they didn't open)
- Wrong people (cheap hosts don't invest)
- Bad timing (hit them again tomorrow)

**What to do:**

**Tomorrow (Day 3):**

1. **Send 50 more emails** with different subject line
2. **Follow up** with original 150 (different angle)
3. **Target richer hosts** (property price > $200/night)
4. **Try different cities** (some convert better)
5. **Keep going for 1 week minimum**

**Stats reality:**
- 3-5% of cold outreach converts
- So 200 emails = 6-10 conversions
- 200 emails × 35% open rate = 70 opens
- 70 opens × 5-10% click rate = 3-7 clicks
- 3-7 clicks × 30-40% conversion = 1-3 sales

**Timeline:**
- Day 1-2: 150 emails sent, 0-1 sales (fine)
- Day 3-4: 300 emails sent, 2-4 sales (great!)
- Day 5-7: 500 emails sent, 5-10 sales ($100-200)

**Patience. It compounds.**
