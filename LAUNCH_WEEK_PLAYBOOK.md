## DAY 1: Monday - Foundation Setup (2 hours)

### Task 1: Lead List Creation (30 min)

**Goal:** Find 500 Airbnb hosts to contact

**Option A: Manual (Free)**
```
Go to: airbnb.com
Search: [Any US city]
Sort by: Newest listings (likely less optimized)
Collect: Host names, listing URLs, price, amenities claimed

Why: Newer hosts are more likely to have poor photos
Collect: 50-100 manually, outsource rest
```

**Option B: Automated Scraping (Cost: $50)**
```
Tools to use:
  - Airbnb Data Scraper (fiverr, $50)
  - Hunter.io (reverse email lookup)
  - Apollo.io (email finder)
  
Output: 500 hosts with:
  - Email
  - Property URL
  - Price point
  - Amenities claimed
  - Location
```

**Best cities to target (most properties, highest prices):**
1. New York (luxury = higher willingness to pay)
2. Los Angeles (seasonal tourism)
3. San Francisco (remote workers, premium pricing)
4. Austin (growing market, tech-savvy hosts)
5. Denver (mountain tourism, activities like hot tubs)

**Recommendation:** Spend 3 hours manually collecting 200 emails + outsource 300 for $50

**Expected outcome:** 500 qualified leads by end of Day 1

## DAY 4: Thursday - Setup Payment (1 hour)

### Task 1: Connect Stripe (30 min)

**Goal:** Get paid when someone buys

**Steps:**
1. Create Stripe account (stripe.com)
2. Add bank account
3. Create product: "Listing Gap Analysis" ($20)
4. Get payment link: stripe.com/...
5. Embed link in landing page

**Result:** Customer clicks → Stripe checkout → You get paid

### Task 2: Setup Delivery System (20 min)

**Customer journey:**
```
Customer pays $20
↓
Stripe webhook fires
↓
Email customer: "Thank you! Here's your analysis" [attach PDF]
↓
Save customer email to "Customers" spreadsheet
↓
Day 1 results come back
↓
Follow up: "Your analysis is ready" + "Subscribe for $20/month?"
↓
Close: Either convert to paid service or ask for testimonial
```

**Use Zapier or IFTTT to automate:**
- Stripe payment received → Send email
- Stripe payment received → Add to spreadsheet
- Stripe payment received → Slack notification (so you know in real-time)

**Cost:** Free for first 100, then $25/month

**Expected outcome:** Payment system ready

## DAY 5: Friday - First Sales Push (2 hours)

### Task 1: Send Next 100 Emails (30 min)

**Targeting this time:**
- Higher price properties ($200+/night)
- Properties with 100+ reviews (successful hosts, willing to invest)
- Properties with luxury amenities (hot tub, pool, fireplace)

**Why?** They have higher customer lifetime value. $100 to $200 per property.

### Task 2: Follow Up With Leads (30 min)

**Who replied yesterday?**
- Jump on it. Send them the analysis within 2 hours.
- Offer: Free 15-min consultation to discuss

**Who opened but didn't click?**
- Send email 2 (the value proof version)

**Who clicked but didn't reply?**
- Send email 3 from another address (sometimes people miss emails)

### Task 3: Analyze & Optimize (1 hour)

**Look at your data:**
- Which email subject got highest open rate?
- Which property type is replying most? (apartments, houses, villas?)
- Which city has best conversion rate?

**Double down on what works.**

Example:
- "Subject line X got 45% open rate → use similar lines"
- "Luxury properties (>$200/night) reply 3x more → target only those"
- "NYC properties convert 2x better → focus there first"

**Expected outcome:** 200 emails sent, 2-5 conversions
