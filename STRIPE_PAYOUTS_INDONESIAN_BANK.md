# 💰 STRIPE PAYOUTS TO INDONESIAN BANK ACCOUNT - COMPLETE GUIDE

## Quick Answer

**YES - You can withdraw money from Stripe to your Indonesian bank.**

**Timeline:**
- Set up: 5-10 minutes
- First payout: Instant setup, but Stripe holds funds for 2-7 days (fraud prevention)
- Withdrawal speed: 1-3 business days to Indonesia

---

## Step-by-Step Setup (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Stripe Dashboard
1. Visit: https://dashboard.stripe.com
2. Log in with your account
3. Click **Settings** (gear icon, bottom left)

### Step 2: Select Your Country
1. Click **Account settings** or **Business profile**
2. Set country to: **Indonesia**
3. Save

### Step 3: Add Bank Account

1. Go to **Settings → Bank accounts and transfers**
2. Click **"Add bank account"**
3. Select: **Manually enter account details**

### Step 4: Enter Indonesian Bank Details

**You'll need:**
- Bank name (e.g., BCA, Mandiri, BNI, CIMB)
- Account number (nomor rekening)
- Account holder name (exactly as it appears on bank statement)
- Bank code (if required)

**Example (BCA):**
```
Bank Name: PT Bank Central Asia Tbk
Account Number: 123456789012
Account Holder: FAKHRI ALMUBAROK
Bank Code: 014 (BCA)
```

### Step 5: Verify the Account

Stripe will:
1. Send 2 small deposits to your Indonesian bank (₱0.01-₱1)
2. Wait 1-3 business days for deposits to appear
3. You confirm the amounts in Stripe dashboard
4. Account verified ✅

**Typical timeline: 2-5 business days for verification**

### Step 6: Set Payout Schedule

Once verified:
1. Go to **Settings → Payouts**
2. Set payout frequency:
   - **Daily** (funds every business day)
   - **Weekly** (Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays)
   - **Monthly** (specific date)

**Recommended for you:** Daily (so you get paid every day if you have sales)

### Step 7: Confirm Payment Method

1. Verify bank account shows in **Settings → Bank accounts**
2. Status should show: **Active** or **Verified**
3. Ready to receive payouts ✅

**Per transaction:**
- Card payments (like tomorrow): 2.9% + $0.30 USD
- So if customer pays $20:
  - Stripe takes: $0.88

**Payout to Indonesian bank:**
- Stripe fee: $0 (FREE for Asia)
- Exchange rate: Stripe uses mid-market rate (no markup)
- Bank fee: Your bank may charge IDR 0-50,000 (ask them)

## FAQ

### Q: How long does money take to reach my Indonesian bank?
**A:** 1-3 business days after Stripe sends it. Stripe sends daily if you set daily payout schedule.

### Q: What if my bank rejects the transfer?
**A:** Stripe will retry. If rejected 3x, they hold the money. Contact Stripe support to resolve.

### Q: Do I need a business bank account?
**A:** No, personal account works. But business account is cleaner for taxes.

### Q: What if I'm not verified yet when customer pays tomorrow?
**A:** Money stays in Stripe. Once verified → Payout happens automatically. You don't lose it.

### Q: Can I withdraw partially?
**A:** No, Stripe pays out all available balance on your payout day.

### Q: What if I want daily payouts of $100 minimum?
**A:** Can't set minimum, but you can request manual payouts anytime via support chat.

### Q: Does Stripe charge fees for payout to Indonesia?
**A:** NO - Stripe charges $0 for payouts to Asia. Only your bank might charge you.

### Q: How much will my bank charge?
**A:** Usually IDR 0-50,000 per international transfer. Call your bank.

### Q: Can I receive payment from multiple customers?
**A:** YES - All funds go to same Stripe account → consolidated payout to your bank once per day.
