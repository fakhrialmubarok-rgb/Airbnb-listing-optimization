## Do This NOW (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Find Your Bank Info
- Open your bank app or statement
- Write down: **Account number**
- Write down: **Account holder name**
- Look up: **Bank code** (see table below)

### Step 2: Log Into Stripe
- Go: https://dashboard.stripe.com
- Click: Settings (⚙️ icon)
- Click: "Bank accounts and transfers"

### Step 3: Add Bank Account
- Click: "Add bank account"
- Select: "Manually enter account details"
- Enter your information:
  - Bank name
  - Account number
  - Account holder name
  - Bank code
- Click: Save ✅

### Step 4: Wait for Verification
- Stripe sends 2 small deposits (₱0.01-₱1) to your bank
- Wait 1-3 business days
- Check your bank statement for 2 amounts
- Log back into Stripe and confirm amounts
- Verified ✅
